While this simple and elegant website was realized as a part of a Master course assignement I intend to use this as an online, alternative cv/portfolio. Therefore, I tried to keep it as coherent and complete as possible. I have included a basic description of myself along with my research and professional experience along with my published work.

The aesthetics of this website was largely inspired by these examples:

  • http://getskeleton.com/#intro
  • https://www.w3schools.com/w3css/tryw3css_templates_photo2.htm

    • The navigation bar included is based on the navbar by bootstrap:

  • https://getbootstrap.com/docs/4.0/components/navbar/

    • The setup of the site includes text and photos which is arranged in a grid style:

  • https://www.w3schools.com/css/css_grid.asp

    • For the svg animated objects I chose to add a movable line encircling the links of the navigation bar as the mouse pointer hovers on it. This was inspired by:

  • https://codepen.io/seanmccaffery/pen/kWWxzQ

    • A js script is also included in the Research and Development section which provides a tabbed navigation.

  • https://www.w3schools.com/howto/howto_js_tabs.asp

    • Finally, the Quantum Sensing data-driver figure was found in the public tableau repositories.

  • https://public.tableau.com/en-us/s/
