Research and development: Quantum Sensing

The so-called quantum revolution is happening. The European Union has officially stated its intention to build the first quantum computer. As a result a thriving collaboration between academia and industry is under way to achieve this goal.

Very recenlty, I took up the challenge to kick-off quantum technlogy activities in the framework of a collabotarion between greek SME and greek academia: whilst Greece possesses very high-qualified scientific personel trained in experimental quantum optics, compared to other european countries, there is a lack of an ecosystem where novel quantum technologies could be developed.
